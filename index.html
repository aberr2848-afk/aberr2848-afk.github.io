About

People First.

Fluent in Apple, Windows, and Android. Known for making technology feel like it belongs to the person using it, not the other way around.

Technology has a way of making people feel like outsiders in their own lives. I built a career on changing that. Whether it’s a custom build, a strategic consultation, or a simple conversation, the goal is always the same: make it make sense, make it feel personal, and make sure you walk away feeling confident, not confused.

Curiosity didn’t wait for permission. At 14, while most were just using technology, I was taking it apart and rebuilding it to figure out exactly what made it tick. By 15, that obsession turned into a paycheck. What followed was a career built on solving problems at every level from the hardware in your hands and the video on your screen to the operations that keep an entire organization moving.

Today I bridge the gap between technical complexity and everyday life, using years of experience to ensure your tools empower you to do your best work.
